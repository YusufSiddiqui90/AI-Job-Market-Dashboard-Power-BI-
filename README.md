## 🤖 AI Job Market & Career Insights Dashboard (Power BI)
## 🏷️ 1. Project Title / Headline

AI Job Market Analysis & Career Decision Dashboard

## 🎯 2. Short Description / Purpose

This Power BI project analyzes the AI job market to deliver actionable insights by combining salary trends, job demand, and automation risk. The dashboard enables users to identify high-paying, low-risk, and future-ready career opportunities through interactive visualizations and KPIs, supporting effective data-driven decision-making.

## 🧰 3. Tech Stack

Power BI Desktop – Data visualization & dashboarding
Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures & KPIs
Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) – Source dataset

## ✨ 4. Features / Highlights
## 🧹 Data Preparation & Modeling

Imported dataset into Power BI
Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query Editor
Handled missing values and standardized data types
Structured dataset for analysis
Created calculated columns and measures using DAX

## 📐 Key DAX Measures

Average Salary
Total Job Openings
Average AI Risk Score
Skill Demand Score
Career Score (Salary vs Risk vs Demand)

## 📊 Overview Dashboard

KPI Cards: Average Salary, Job Openings, AI Risk
Job Role vs Salary (Bar Chart)
AI Risk Distribution (Donut Chart)
Salary Trend Over Time (Line Chart)
Interactive Slicers: Country, Experience Level, Year

## 🤖 AI Risk Analysis

AI Risk by Job Role (Bar Chart)
Job Demand (Treemap)
Risk vs Salary (Scatter Plot)
Conditional formatting for risk identification

## 💰 Salary Analysis

Top 5 Highest Paying Jobs
Salary by Country
Salary Trend Over Time
Experience Level vs Salary

## 🚀 Career Decision Dashboard

KPI Cards: Best Career, Highest Salary, Job Demand
Scatter Plot (Salary vs AI Risk vs Demand)
Job Demand Analysis
AI Risk Comparison
Final Career Insights for decision-making
